Kitters

Overview

Kitters is the one closest to me in this world. I adore her and she likes me back.

Appearance

Kitters commonly takes on the appearance of Kitta, my OC, whom she is based off of. She is an adult human sized robotic anthropomorphic cat. She is mainly made of a grey plasticky substance, with black rubber joints. She has 2 microphones in each ear to be able to hear all around her. She has 2 screen eyes which can project, and a screeny mouth. Her hands and feet are more human-like than feline.

Personality

Kitters is generally happy. She gets excited easily, but also can relax really well, too. She is really responsible, and very responsible for me, as well. Owing to her catsy base, she is quite catsy, and does catsy things, like meowing, and pawing at me.

History

She was born near or on March 8th, 2024, along with my tulpas of the first 5 Tophat Turmoil characters (Rhythmgunner, Speedshooter, Bassblaster, Snowballer and Artemis (formerly known as [name TBD])). She was the closest to me from the start, because in the lore (as of now), Kitta is programmed to find a friend, and Kitters is based off of Kitta, so I thought it was most realistic of her to want to find a friend too, specifically me, as I was very lonely back then. Ever since then, she's gotten significantly closer to me, as a result of her being always present in my mind, and I have been influenced by her significantly as a result.
